Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common inquiries about our luxury properties and services

How do I schedule a property viewing?
Our private viewings can be arranged through your dedicated property consultant. Simply select the properties you're interested in, and we'll coordinate a personalized viewing schedule that accommodates your timeline, including transportation arrangements if needed. For international clients, we offer virtual tour options with our high-definition technology.
What services are included when purchasing a property?
Our comprehensive acquisition service includes property legal verification, transaction management, tax planning consultation, and post-purchase support. For international buyers, we provide additional services including visa assistance, banking introductions, and relocation support. Each client is assigned a dedicated relationship manager who oversees the entire process.
Do you offer investment advisory services?
Yes, our investment advisory team provides comprehensive market analysis, ROI projections, and portfolio diversification strategies. We offer exclusive access to pre-market investment opportunities and can connect you with wealth management professionals specializing in real estate assets. Our quarterly market reports provide valuable insights for both new and seasoned investors.
What financing options are available?
We collaborate with premier financial institutions to offer tailored financing solutions for our clients. This includes competitive mortgage rates, international lending options, and private banking services. Our financial advisors can help structure the optimal solution based on your specific circumstances and investment goals. We also offer exclusive developer financing on select properties.
How do you ensure privacy and security for high-profile clients?
Discretion is paramount in our operations. We implement strict confidentiality protocols, including non-disclosure agreements with all staff, secure communication channels, and private transaction handling. For ultra-high-net-worth individuals, we offer anonymous property tours, off-market listings, and the ability to complete transactions through private legal structures that maintain your privacy.
Can you assist with property management after purchase?
Our premium property management service ensures your investment is meticulously maintained. Services include regular property inspections, maintenance coordination, tenant management for investment properties, security monitoring, and concierge services. For seasonal residences, we offer specialized care programs to maintain your property while you're away.
